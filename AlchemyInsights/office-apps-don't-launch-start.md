- [Zbadaj możliwe problemy z dodatkami](https://support.office.com/article/powerpoint-isn-t-responding-hangs-or-freezes-652ede6e-e3d2-449a-a07f-8c800dfb948d#bkmk_addins) zainstalowanymi dla pakietu Office

- [Odinstaluj](https://support.office.com/article/uninstall-office-from-a-pc-9dd49b83-264a-477a-8fcc-2fdf5dbf61d8) i ponownie zainstaluj pakiet Office
